**Clan Ladon** was a nomadic clan of dragonborn barbarians in the Scorching Sands. They vie for power in [[2. The World/Locations/The Verdant Reach\|The Verdant Reach]] against their fiercest rivals, [[2. The World/Organisations/The Russett Pride\|The Russett Pride]].

## History
Initially reluctant to join the war, the Clan ultimately committed to joining [[2. The World/Locations/The Kingdom of Alastia\| Alastia]] in [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\| the Sundering War]] after realizing that the Pride had joined the Cer'Dorei Republic.
