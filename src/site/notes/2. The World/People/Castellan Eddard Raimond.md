**Castellan Eddard Raimond** is a military officer in the [[2. The World/Organisations/The Victorian Revolutionary Guard\|The Victorian Revolutionary Guard]] and the commander of [[2. The World/Locations/Summer's Outpost\|Summer's Outpost]]. In his time working to civilize the Jungle, he also serves as a foster father and mentor to Lieutenant Wang Lei. 

A dedicated and loyal man, he served honorably as a squire under the General [[2. The World/People/Victoria Parcival\|Victoria Parcival]] during the [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\|The Sundering War]]. He assisted her in the raid against Coatlan, which saw the liberation of the surviving members of House Xicoatl who had been held against their will by Cer'Dorei forces.

Notably, he is a devout follower of Caishenye, the god of prosperity.

## The Graying
In 790 AY, upon the onset of the widespread curse known as the Graying, the Victorian Empress begun commissioning expeditions into the jungle continent of Kroonstad to locate and destroy the necromantic device known as the Devourer, which was believed to be the source of the curse. Many of such expeditions were unsuccessful, with almost all going into the jungle vanishing.

Under the advisement of the [[2. The World/Organisations/Chamber of Lords-Advisory\|Chamber of Lords-Advisory]], the Lieutenant of Summer's Outpost and his foster son Wang Lei was selected to be part of [[1. The Party/0. The Party\|the 52nd Expedition]]. Believing this is a good opportunity to boost his portfolio and stage Wang Lei to take over as Castellan of Summer's Outpost, Eddard encouraged the Lieutenant to take on this quest and succeed.

Despite this, the Castellan was notably upset about the replacement for Deputy Commander. Noting that the garrison still needs a second-in-command, he shared that the Chamber of Lords-Advisory has sent the fresh Lieutenant Henry Thia to stand-in in Wang Lei's absence. While necessary, the Castellan expressed disapproval as Henry Thia was formerly removed from command after getting caught stealing gems from the Russett mines. Nonetheless, the pair resolved to make the best of the situation, and sought to finish the mission as quickly as possible to minimize this transition period.
