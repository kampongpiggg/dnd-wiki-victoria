Professor Hermione McGonagall is an Abjuration Professor of the [[2. The World/Organisations/Hexeverel\|Hexeverel]], and serves as a mentor to Salazar Black. A powerful Abjuration wizard, she spends her time guiding the best and brightest at the prestigious university. She believed in that magic and philosophy should be widely available to all, and that education should not be commodified. She wanted to educate as many of the commonfolk as possible, and to do so without charging excessive tuition.

## The Graying
Having been a seasoned adventurer herself, the Professor was afflicted by the widespread curse known as [[2. The World/History/The Graying\|The Graying]]. When the Eyes of the Emperor identified that the source of the curse was a necromantic device known as the Devourer located in Kroonstad, [[2. The World/Organisations/The Victorian Revolutionary Guard\|The Victorian Revolutionary Guard]] began commissioning expeditions to locate and destroy the device. 

Salazar Black, her mentee and longtime friend, was commissioned to be part of [[1. The Party/0. The Party\|the 52nd Expedition]]. In his journey to join the Expedition, he stopped by the Hexeverel to meet her. In the fear of her impending death, she offers him one last homework, the book known as *Shadow of the Whispered One: The Formative Years of the Architect.*
