**Queen Gwendolyn Hamilton** was the queen consort of [[2. The World/Locations/The Kingdom of Alastia\|The Kingdom of Alastia]] and the wife of the late [[2. The World/People/Arthur Hamilton\|Arthur Hamilton]], who was assassinated by elven assassins at the behest of [[2. The World/People/Julian Yaeldrin\| Senator Julian Yaeldrin]] and [[2. The World/People/Ovina Torunn\| Chancellor Ovina Torunn]]. 

Despite being next in the line of succession and thus the rightful queen, she was denied the throne by [[2. The World/People/Eleanor Hamilton\| the Dowager Queen Eleanor Hamilton]].
