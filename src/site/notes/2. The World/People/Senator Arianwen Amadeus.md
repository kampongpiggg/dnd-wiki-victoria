Senator Arianwen Amadeus is a member of [[2. The World/Organisations/The Cervine Senate\|The Cervine Senate]] and mother to Vilde Amadeus. A shrewd politician, Arianwen has risen to power through meritorious service and a campaign predicated on progressivism and socialism. Her most notable policies included grain subsidies, substantial judicial reforms and, controversially, redistribution of public land to landless citizens. Her focus on combating the social inequality between the high elves and the wood elves has made her a contentious figure in the Senate. 

## The Graying
In 790 AY, upon the onset of the widespread curse known as the Graying, [[2. The World/Nations/The Victorian Empire\|The Victorian Empire]] began researching for the cause. When it was learnt by [[2. The World/Organisations/The Victorian Revolutionary Guard\|The Victorian Revolutionary Guard]] that the source of the Graying was a necromantic device known as the Devourer located in Kroonstad, Empress [[2. The World/People/Victoria Parcival\|Victoria Parcival]] began commissioning expeditions into Kroonstad to locate and destroy the device. Many of such expeditions were unsuccessful, with almost all going into the jungle vanishing.

Under the advisement of the [[2. The World/Organisations/Chamber of Lords-Advisory\|Chamber of Lords-Advisory]], the Senator was nominated to be the leader of the [[1. The Party/0. The Party\|52nd Expedition]]. Unfortunately, her imperial summons was stolen by her daughter Vilde Amadeus, who used it to join the Expedition in her stead.
