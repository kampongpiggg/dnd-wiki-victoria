**Vaxildan and Vexalia** were a pair of elven assassins who served [[2. The World/Organisations/The High Syndicacy\| the High Syndicacy]] of [[2. The World/Locations/The Rarquet Assembly\| the Rarquet Assembly]]. Their assassination of the King of Alastia set off a chain of events that would lead to the [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\|The Sundering War]]. Born as identical twins, they were abandoned by their parents at birth and taken in by [[2. The World/People/Ovina Torunn\|Ovina Torunn]] who trained them to serve her interests.

In 749 AY, under the orders of Chancellor Ovina to increase profits, the twins snuck into [[2. The World/Locations/The Kingdom of Alastia\|The Kingdom of Alastia]] and assassinated King [[2. The World/People/Arthur Hamilton\|Arthur Hamilton]]. This incited a succession crisis as the Dowager Queen Eleanor Hamilton seized the throne of Alastia over the rightful heir, the half-elf Queen Gwendolyn Hamilton. Tensions arose between Alastia and Cer'Dorei as a result of the crisis, eventually boiling over into the Sundering War.
