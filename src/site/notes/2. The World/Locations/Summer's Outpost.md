**Summer's Outpost** is the primary Imperial garrison in [[2. The World/Nations/The Verdant Reach\|the Verdant Reach]]. Commanded by Castellan Eddard Raimond, it is one of the smaller garrisons constructed by the [[2. The World/Organisations/The Victorian Revolutionary Guard\| Victorian Revolutionary Guard]] after [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\|The Sundering War]] and the formation of the [[2. The World/Nations/The Victorian Empire\|Victorian Empire]].

## Description
Built on a massive ridgetop overlooking the Russett Jungle, Summer's Outpost is a stone motte-and-bailey castle that houses the armory, training grounds, and barracks for the local Victorian Revolutionary Guard. As part of their primary mandate to protect the Concord and the Empress, the day-to-day business of the Castle is largely concerned with enforcing the Empire's laws, supporting the rule of [[2. The World/People/War Chief Hralvok\|War Chief Hralvok]], and the prevention of international conflict.

While small, the castle was of strategic significance to the Victorian Empire. The Russett Jungle produces almost half of the Empire's gems, which were utilized frequently as spellcasting components (in particular, the diamonds required for resurrection spells). The continued production of such gems were integral to the spellcasting of the Empire.

## The Graying
In 790 AY, upon the onset of the widespread curse known as the Graying, Empress [[2. The World/People/Victoria Parcival\|Victoria Parcival]] begun commissioning expeditions into the jungle continent of Kroonstad to locate and destroy the necromantic device known as the Devourer. Many of such expeditions were unsuccessful, with almost all going into the jungle vanishing.

Under the advisement of the [[2. The World/Organisations/Chamber of Lords-Advisory\|Chamber of Lords-Advisory]], the Lieutenant of Summer's Outpost, Wang Lei, was selected to be part of the [[1. The Party/0. The Party\|52nd Expedition]]. In his stead, the Chamber of Lords-Advisory sent the fresh Lieutenant Henry Thia to stand-in as Deputy Commander of Summer's Outpost.
