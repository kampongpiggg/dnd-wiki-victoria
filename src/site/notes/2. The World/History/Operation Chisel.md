**Operation Chisel** was a planned military operation by [[2. The World/Organisations/Force Grey\|Force Grey]], [[2. The World/Locations/Ophidia\|Ophidia]]'s elite military unit. Led by [[Felicity \| the Archmage Felicity]] as a direct response to the [[2. The World/History/Attack on the Kryn Ward\|Attack on the Kryn Ward]], its primary objective was the destruction of forges and smiths capable of producing the devastating automatons used by the [[2. The World/Locations/The Rarquet Assembly\|The Rarquet Assembly]] in [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\|The Sundering War]]. It had a secondary objective in capturing or killing the Rarquet head-of-state, [[Ovina Torunn \| Chancellor Ovina Torunn]] of the [[2. The World/Organisations/The High Syndicacy\|The High Syndicacy]].
