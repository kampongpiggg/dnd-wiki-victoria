**The Attack on the Kryn Ward** was a terrorist attack that occured in the capital of Ophidia in 752 AY. A runepowder bomb that was planted in the basement of the Black Hound, a local tavern in [[2. The World/Locations/The Kryn Ward\|The Kryn Ward]], exploded, instantly killing 52 and injuring 72 others. The district was damaged severely by the bomb. The bomb, planted by [[The High Syndicacy \| Syndicacy]] agents [[2. The World/People/Velissa & Vexalia\|Velissa & Vexalia]] on the orders of [[Ovina Torunn \| Chancellor Ovina Torunn]], led to the death of the Archmage [[2. The World/People/Maribel Merewyn\|Maribel Merewyn]] and [[2. The World/Locations/Ophidia\|Ophidia]]'s entry into [[2. The World/History/The Sundering War\|The Sundering War]].
